About the National Musabaqah

The National Qur’anic Recitation Competition, initiated in 1986, is the brainchild of the Centre for Islamic Studies, Usman Danfodiyo University, Sokoto. The first competitions were held in Sokoto under the university's supervision, and since then, it has become an annual national event hosted by various states across the federation.

The event brings together youths from nearly every state in Nigeria, including the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), who have memorized the Qur’an. Winners at the national level often represent Nigeria in international competitions in countries like Saudi Arabia and Malaysia. Nigeria has proudly secured first-place positions multiple times.
